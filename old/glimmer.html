What is Glimmer?

You may be wondering why this is called the Glimmer guide. Glimmer is the Grinnell Laboratory for Interactive Multimedia Research and Experimentation. Since a primary target of this work is my research students, it made sense to name it for my lab. (That also means that my research students will be encouraged to work on it.)
